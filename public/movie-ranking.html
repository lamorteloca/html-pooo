The Best Movies According To Ricardo

My top 5 movies of all time.

5 Tropic Thunder

Genre: Comedy / Action / Satire / Adventure

Packed full of hilarious and infinitely quotable lines, it also features two scene-stealing performances by Matthew McConaughey and Tom Cruise Donkey Reviews

4 The Shawshank Redemption

Genre: Drama / Mistery

A beloved, incessantly revisited, stone-cold classic Tomatoes News

3 Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back

Genre: Sci-Fi / Action / Fantasy / Adventure

Dark, sinister, but ultimately even more involving than A New Hope, The Empire Strikes Back defies viewer expectations and takes the series to heightened emotional levels Amazing Spidey Reviews

2 The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

Genre: Fantasy / Adventure / Drama / Action

Visually breathtaking and emotionally powerful, The Lord of the Rings - The Return of the King is a moving and satisfying conclusion to a great trilogy The Made Up Journal

1 Pulp Fiction

Genre: Thriller / Drama / Comedy / Crime

One of the most influential films of the 1990s, Pulp Fiction is a delirious post-modern mix of neo-noir thrills, pitch-black humor, and pop-culture touchstones King Kong Review
